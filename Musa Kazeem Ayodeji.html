Get to know Musa Kazeem Ayodeji

Full Name: Kazeem Ayodeji Musa

Parents: Ayuba Musa Olukotun

Birth Date/Place: 1417 AH/810 CE (Muharram 14) (Kaduna)

Kazeem was born and brought up in Kaduna State, Nigeria. He is the first child and the only son in a family of five. He began his studies, both in Islamic and Western Education in Kaduna State where he was born until he graduated from secondary school. Afterwhich, he began studies at Maritime Academy of Nigeria, Oron, Akwa Ibom State, where he studied Nautical Science and obtained a Higher National Diploma. He is currently married to A'ishah and has a daughter named Sumayyah. He is also a martial artist and a self-defense expert and has taught a number of students.
